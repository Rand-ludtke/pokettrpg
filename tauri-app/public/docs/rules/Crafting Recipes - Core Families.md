## Family notes

### Pokemon Medicine
- this is the core answer to the new travel-healing rules
- Potion, Super, Hyper, Max, Revive, and Full Restore style items should live here

### Human Medicine
- keeps trainers from feeling immortal on the road
- best used as limited route sustain, not a replacement for a hospital or Center

### Food
- should help with travel, social scenes, stamina pacing, and soft healing without replacing shops

### Lures & Bait
- use this family to shape encounter tone, not just raw rarity
- Basic, Good, Great, and Ultra Lures should feel like a clear progression in range and pull

### Traps
- best when they solve a positioning problem rather than just deal damage

### Expedition Gear
- this is where route identity, camp quality, and mounted travel really matter

### Tech Tools
- excellent for Rocket and city arcs

### Seam / Fusion Gear
- strongest when treated as controlled, scarce, and slightly dangerous
