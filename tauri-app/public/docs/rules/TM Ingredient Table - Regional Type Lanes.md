## How to use this table
This is the fast-reference sheet for turning **common regional and route Pokemon** into TM ingredients without stopping play.

For the full regional Sage/CAP/Custom roster, use [TM Ingredient Table - Regional Species Index.md](TM%20Ingredient%20Table%20-%20Regional%20Species%20Index.md).

Default rule:
- a harvested part normally feeds **one of that Pokemon's own types**
- if the Pokemon is dual-type, pick the lane that matches the part being harvested
- if the Pokemon has **Normal**, it can instead be harvested as Normal support for any non-Ghost TM

If a common route mon is not listed here, use this fallback:
- choose its primary type
- assign a logical body part
- give it MV from the normal stage chart
- map it to the closest move lane in [TM Crafting & Etching.md](TM%20Crafting%20%26%20Etching.md)

## Fast adjudication notes
- If a player harvests a part creatively, keep the lane but change the move flavor.
- Dual-type materials are best used to let the party choose between two lanes, not to double-count value.
- If a mon is especially iconic for one move, let its blueprint and ingredient feel naturally linked.

Examples:
- Mareep fur naturally wants Electric work.
- Gligar claws naturally want Ground or Flying work.
- Bleepoat fleece is great for Grass work and can sometimes stabilize Normal support crafts.
- Necronite fossil shards are excellent Rock or Psychic material depending on what part was taken.
