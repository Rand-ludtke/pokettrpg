## Gathering procedure

### Quick mode
At the end of a route segment, award:
- 1 small bundle from a common source
- 1 standard bundle on a successful check
- 1 strong bundle on a very strong success or special encounter

### Detailed mode
1. pick the biome table
2. make one gather check
3. roll or choose a result
4. convert the result into parts or MV bundles

Recommended gather checks:

| Situation | Check |
|---|---|
| spotting useful material | Intelligence |
| safely harvesting it | Intelligence or Fortitude |
| prying, cutting, hauling | Strength |
| climbing or crossing to reach it | Athletics |
| scavenging tech or salvage | Intelligence |

## Fishing procedure

Fishing is a route action that usually replaces one normal gather attempt for that half-day.

1. choose the water scene: calm pond, river, coast, canal, sewer, storm surf, reef, deep lake, and so on
2. pick the rod tier being used
3. make the fishing check
4. on a success, roll on that rod's table for the current water biome
5. on a strong success, either roll twice and keep one result or move one step up the same table at DM discretion

Recommended fishing checks:

| Situation | Check |
|---|---|
| calm casting, line control, fighting the pull | Athletics |
| reading water, timing the cast, spotting signs | Intelligence |
| brute-forcing a heavy catch ashore | Strength |
| soothing a skittish or bond-sensitive target | Speech |

Fishing DC by conditions:

| Water condition | DC |
|---|---:|
| calm pond, dock, stocked canal | 8 |
| river, normal lake, beach surf | 10 |
| reef shelf, storm chop, marsh tangle, sewer runoff | 12 |
| fracture water, deep trench, high-chaos current | 14+ |

Rod rules:
- Old Rod can only roll its own table and usually cannot hook apex or evolved predators unless the route explicitly says otherwise.
- Basic Rod is the standard all-purpose rod and can fish almost any ordinary table.
- Good Rod can roll its own table or downgrade to Basic Rod when the party wants steadier food catches.
- Deluxe Rod can roll its own table or downgrade to Good Rod; on a very strong success it can upgrade one result into the route's rare catch.
- Lures, bait, weather, or route tags can bias the result within the table instead of replacing the rod tier.
